About-Us

Asmara Garage is a full-service auto body repair shop,located in 123 Semaetat st, Asmara, Eritrea.We are a brothers-owned business and have been in operation since 2001. We have a combined experience of over 50 years in the auto body repair industry. We give a full service to our customers, from the moment they walk in the door to the moment they drive away in their newly repaired vehicle. We are happy to solve any problem you may have with your vehicle, and we are always happy to help you with any questions you may have about your vehicle.
